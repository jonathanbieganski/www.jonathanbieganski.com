About Me

I started my tech career in 2005 as a member of the Microservices team at Computer Assiociates, which is now known as CA Technologies. This was where I wrote my first professional internal tool written in Visual basic. That was also the first time I had written a program outside of academia and more importantly, the first time I received professional feedback and grattitude for coding ingenuity. I was hooked, bit and forever changed by the coding bug.

After the housing market crash of 2008 and the extensive layoffs witnessed firsthand in 2010, I took the expereince I gained at CA Technologies and ventured forth to apply my skills elsewhere.

I landed an Instructional Design position at the local community college where I was a student.

I left school to care for an ailing family member and picked up a few projects troubleshooting systems, setting up networks and security systems. Around this time I met an amazing EdTech Sales Manager and eventually made her my wife. She motivated me to continue my pursuetes and dream to work Silicon Valley. The Lead Systems Administrator at icontrol Networks, which was absorbed by Alarm.com, saw something in me and brought me in under his wing. I learned about Tier 1 server updates, Atlassian Suite permissions, print management, VOIP and Cisco managed switch configuration. I also completed my very first Microsoft Certification: Enabling and Managing M365 (M20347).

With extensive experience in the tech industry, I specialize in Azure Cloud Services and automated solutions. I've worked in diverse corporate settings. Please look around and feel free to contact and connect with me.
