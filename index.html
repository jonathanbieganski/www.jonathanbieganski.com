About:

My tech career began in 2005 at CA Technologies on their Microservices team. For the first time I was granted access to production environments! There were Windows, Unix and Linux systems, clusters and hypervisors. I programmed my first internal tool for my team while working there, sparking my passion for problem-solving and coding.

After the housing market crash and the following recession I began my formal studies in computer science and ethical hacking at City College of San Francisco. While attending I worked as an Instructional Designer in their Technology Learning Center. I trained professors and Lab Aides to construct Canvas and Moodle courses. I managed the systems. This experience taught me many valuable lessons about academia's unique needs and challenges.

I briefly stepped away from my studies and design career to care for a family member, taking on projects when possible. It was during this time that I met an amazing EdTech Sales Manager. I eventually convinced her to marry me. She played a pivotal role in helping me redirect my focus and pursue my ambition of working in tech within Silicon Valley.

The Lead Systems Administrator at iControl Networks recognized my potential and brought me onboard. There I gained experience in various systems and earned my first Microsoft Certification. Alarm.com absorded icontrol networks. I began to specialize in Azure, M365, Windows Server and macOS. The high cost of living in Silicon Valley led me to resign and relocate to Sacramento where lined up a junior System Administrator position at Magnit.

After some time at Magnit managing their systems and writing some PowerShell scripts I was offered a System Administrator role by IT Director of RSN Technologies. There I managed a small team and managed mutiple tenants and systems. At the very beginning of the covid pandemic, when lockdowns began, circumstances changed, we sold our home in Sacramento and moved to Oregon.

After settling in to our new property in Oregon I began work at Fisher Phillips. This is my current employer and my role is quite varied. I manage Microsoft System Center Configuration Manager & Microsoft Endpoint Configuration Manager, lead projects, stand up systems, create workflows and write automation.

With numerous years of experience and a diverse background in tech, coupled with expertise in Microsoft Azure Cloud Services and the M365 Stack, I am eager to engage in conversations and explore fresh opportunities. I bring a wealth of valuable skills and insights that can be of great benefit.
